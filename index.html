Willow Branch Kennel, established in 1998, is located in rural Cisco, IL. Our motto is "The Best for Your Pet." We strive to provide a boarding environment that is clean, healthy and secure and that provides adequate exercise and personalized care. We offer 42 individual indoor/outdoor runs and twelve fenced exercise yards. All dogs are provided two outside run times daily. When weather and temperature allow, the dogs also have access to their outside patio throughout the day. The building offers central heat and air conditioning as well as radiant floor heat during the cooler months.

While the majority of our clients are of the canine variety, we also offer cat boarding in a room off the office dedicated to feline guests.

Our goal is to make your pet look forward to his stay with us and to make you, the owner, at ease to enjoy your time away without worrying about your pet. As a home based business, owners live on-site. Because we operate the kennel with a limited staff, we become well-acquainted with your pet and offer individual attention to each guest.

We are licensed by the Illinois Department of Agriculture. In compliance with our license, all boarding guests must furnish current proof of immunizations.

Reservations are required. During the summer, holidays and spring break, the kennel is often fully booked three to six weeks in advance. In trying to keep it simple, we don't ask for a deposit but do ask for your personal courtesy and consideration to let us know about changes in your travel plans.

We welcome your pet to "Camp Willow Branch" and look forward to each visit.
